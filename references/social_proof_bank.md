## Usage Notes

- **Always anonymize**: Use descriptors like "a KSA-based 3PL", "a regional
  retail firm", "a Gulf e-commerce fulfillment center" — never real company names.
- **Match the metric to the prospect's role**: Operations leads care about
  time saved; finance leads care about error rates and cost; procurement
  leads care about cycle times.
- **Use only one proof point per message** — keep the message tight.
- **Do not stack claims** — one specific, credible result is more persuasive
  than multiple vague ones.
